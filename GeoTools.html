GeoTools

Geology tools and utilities for QGIS

contact: Roland Hill, roland.hill@mmg.com

Functions

  • Desurvey holes, project drill hole traces to surface and create surface projects of down hole data
  • Reverse the direction of lines. This is needed when using non-symmetric line symbology (eg normal faults)
  • Transparent black or white pixels for all selected images (great for EM channels, remote sensing channels etc)
  • Search and replace drive letter in a Project (for when you move to another hard disk)
